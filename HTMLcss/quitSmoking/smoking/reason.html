Why do you need to quit smoking?

Smoking causes damage to nearly every organ in the body and is directly responsible for a number of diseases.
Every year, more than 480,000 people die in the United States (U.S.) due to tobacco-related diseases.That is around 1
in 5 of all deaths in the U.S. annually.It is estimated that 1 in 2 smokers will die from a smoking-related disease.
